Paying for a tree permit:

Typical review time for a tree permit is 10 working days or less. You will be notified when the permit is approved pending payment of the $31.35 permit fee and will receive payment instructions. Note: The Tree Permit Fee will be increasing to $31.95 on July 1st, 2025.
